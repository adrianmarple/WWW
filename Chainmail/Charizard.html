Charizard

This piece is my attempt to test how well finer details come out. Fortunately, pokemon in the old gameboy pokemon games only use 4 colors. This Charizard specifically has white, black, red, and orange (rendered in galvanized steel, dark annealed steel, copper, and brass respectively).

I think the details came out wonderfully. In fact this is still one of my favorite pieces. It is very similar to the fleur de lis in scope: 6498 rings, uses a 4:1 weave.
