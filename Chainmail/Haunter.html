Haunter

Yes, another pokemon. This Haunter was a crutial test of my ability to work with the anodized titanium wire I got from The Ring Lord. Besides what has become the standard steel and aluminum wire, this piece encorporates "violet" and "rose" colored anodized titanium and I think the effect works quite well.

The only feature that didn't come across right were Haunter's pupils, which I merely removed (replaced with aluminum rings).
