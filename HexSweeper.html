Instructions

Left click to clear a space. Right click to toggle a marker on an uncleared space. Drag to move the entire map. Colored numbers indicate the total number of adjacent mines (if this agrees with the number of adjacent markers, left click to clear all adjacent unmarked spaces). White numbers indicate the remaining number of mines a distance of two away. Either click the HexSweeper logo or press space to restart the game.
